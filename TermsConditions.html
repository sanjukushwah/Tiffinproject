1. Service Overview

SpicyFeast delivers Veg, Non-Veg, and Hybrid tiffin meals across the GTA. Weekly, monthly, and custom plans are available depending on your area.

2. Ordering & Delivery

  • Orders must be placed through our website or official WhatsApp number.
  • Deliveries are made within your selected delivery window.
  • Delays may occur due to traffic, weather, or high-volume days.
  • If you are unavailable during delivery, the tiffin may be left at a safe spot with proof of delivery.

3. Meal Plans & Changes

  • Veg, Non-Veg, and Hybrid meals follow a weekly schedule.
  • Menu items may change depending on ingredient availability.
  • Allergies must be communicated before subscribing.
  • Special requests depend on availability and additional charges.

4. Payments & Billing

  • Payments are made in advance for weekly or monthly plans.
  • Only digital payments are accepted (Stripe/PayPal/Razorpay).
  • Prices may vary based on region, distance, and meal type.
  • Plans are non-refundable once started, unless a service issue occurs.

5. Cancellations & Refunds

You may cancel before your plan starts for a full refund. After the plan begins, refunds are not available, but you may request a plan hold (subject to approval).

6. Food Quality & Safety

Meals are prepared daily in hygienic kitchens. If you receive spoiled or damaged food, report it within 2 hours for a replacement.

7. User Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate delivery information.
  • Be available during the delivery window.
  • Store food safely after delivery.

8. Limitation of Liability

  • Delays due to weather or traffic.
  • Allergic reactions if allergies were not disclosed.
  • Issues after food is delivered.

Our liability is limited to the value of the meal.
